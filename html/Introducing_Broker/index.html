Introducing Broker

When users register the platform, the system will assign a broker randomly to them according to their locations. The users can consult the brokers immediately if they have any questions about forex investment.

All brokers in the system are derived from high-level users of the platform. PHION adheres to the principle that "Able men are in high position ", trains brokers from high-level members, who enjoy the additional broker treatment of the platform.

As a user

As a user, you will be rewarded if you recommend a new user to PHION. The user, who successfully recommends PHION's online trading system to potential investors, will take part of the presentee's profits as a return whenever the customer they recommend trades on PHION's platform.

PHION pays attention to the development of the team and provides attractive promotion mechanism and flexible return mechanism for the platform users. According to the introduced customer volume and transaction quota, the more customers recommended, the higher the volume of transactions, the higher the probability of promotion, and the higher the profits, therefore the referral's commission will also be higher.

Fund Manager

There is only one international senior fund manager on PHION platform currently, who's in charge of the customer investment at the early stage and training fund managers for the platform later.

PHION adheres to keeping the transparency of the platform funds, and will later develop fund managers from high-level users of the platform, that is, enjoying 40% of the customers gains except for the superior partners bonus part.
