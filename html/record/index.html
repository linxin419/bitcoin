Founder's Resume

  • Scott got a master's degree

    Scott graduated from University of London in 1975, with a master's degree in financial management. During school days, he was chosen as a student representative to Harvard University for one year study because of his excellent learning ability and communication skills. After returning to school, Scott created a financial research team led by himself along with students majoring in the same field.

  • Scott was awarded the Most Thoughtful Character of the British Financial Association

    Scott was awarded the Most Thoughtful Character of the British Financial Association in the UK Financial Research Institute in 1983. The award was like east wind to Scott, who did not rest on his laurels and wanted to make a breakthrough at that time, and accelerated Scott's thought to found a company.

  • Scott founded UKPG in 1986

    Scott founded UKPG in 1986, and was recognized by the British financial industry in only four years. He won the Excellence Award of British Financial Investment in 1990, and the Ten Most Influential Characters of British Financial Industry in 2011 again. At this point Scott almost reached the peak of his career, but he did not continue developing steadily. In an opportunity he came into contact with forex and saw the market prospect, then he transformed the development direction of UKPG resolutely. Finally, Scott founded PHION Financial Holding Group Co., Ltd (PHION) in 2017, broke through himself and reached career peak again.
